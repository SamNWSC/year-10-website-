The Visa Cash App RB Formula One Team, previously known as Scuderia AlphaTauri, is a rebranded F1 team competing in the 2024 season. This rebranding reflects a significant sponsorship deal with Visa, a global digital payment network, and Cash App, a platform for money exchange including cryptocurrency. The team, which has its roots in the former Minardi team, has been part of the Red Bull family since 2006, initially serving as a junior team before transitioning to a sister team status. For the 2024 season, the team fields drivers Daniel Ricciardo and Yuki Tsunoda, with Laurent Mekies as the team principal. Despite some criticism over its name change, the team aims to enhance its competitiveness in F1 with a new identity and a bold vision for the future

Yuki Tsunoda

Image Credit: Wikimedia Commons

Yuki Tsunoda, born on May 11, 2000, in Sagamihara, Kanagawa, Japan, is a professional racing driver currently competing in Formula One for the RB Formula One Team. A product of the Honda Formula Dream Project, Tsunoda has been supported by Honda since 2016 and received backing from Red Bull in 2019. He claimed the Japanese F4 Championship in 2018 and finished third in the 2020 Formula 2 Championship, earning him the Anthoine Hubert Award. Tsunoda made his Formula One debut in 2021 with AlphaTauri and has since been recognized for his development as a driver, outperforming his more experienced teammate, Daniel Ricciardo, in the early races of the 2024 season. Despite a reputation for his fiery temper, Tsunoda has shown growth as a leader within his team and continues to establish himself as a formidable competitor in the sport
